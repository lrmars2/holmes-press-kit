EVENT 05 of 33

🏛 Late 2023  |  ADMINISTRATIVE

Register's 88-Page MREC Response — Critical Admission

Daniel Register files an 88-page response to the MREC complaint (R. 15–103). In a pivotal sworn admission, Register states that he AND Northrop Realty were "assigned to manage the Property by Shellpoint Mortgage Servicing." This admission directly links Shellpoint → Register → Northrop in a documented chain of assignment. Register also submits Plaintiff's personal lease agreement from a different address (R. 29–67, 38 pages) and rental payment history (R. 73–79) — documents whose origin Register never explains.

Status: Filed

EVENT 06 of 33

🔴 December 21, 2023  |  ADMINISTRATIVE  |  CRITICAL EVENT

MREC Final Order — Complaint and Guaranty Fund Claim Dismissed

MREC Interim Executive Director Raquel Meyers signs the Final Order dismissing both the disciplinary complaint and the $250,000 Guaranty Fund claim WITHOUT a contested case hearing. The Commission conducts only a paper review. The Order accepts Register's account, ignores the active bankruptcy stay, never explains how Register obtained Plaintiff's personal lease records, and concludes Register was "hired to oversee the property by legal means." The dismissal letter is cc'd to Nick D'Ambrosia, Register's supervising broker at Northrop Realty.

Status: Order Issued

Civil Complaint Filed — Case No. C-24-CV-25-008195

Plaintiff files the civil complaint in Baltimore City Circuit Court against seven defendants: Daniel Register, Northrop Realty / The Creig Northrop Team P.C., Long & Foster Real Estate, HomeServices of America (HSoA), Maryland Association of REALTORS (MAR), Maryland Real Estate Commission (MREC), and McCalla Raymer Leibert Pierce LLC.
