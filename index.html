EVENT 01 of 33

🔴 February – August 2022 | ADMINISTRATIVE | CRITICAL EVENT

Register Listed Home for Sale and Changes Locks on Bankruptcy-Protected Property

Daniel Register of Northrop Realty lists Plaintiff’s home for sale in February 2022. During an active federal bankruptcy stay under 11 U.S.C. §362, Register changes the locks on Plaintiff’s property at 2831 E. Northern Parkway, Baltimore, MD — without her knowledge or consent. A sign falsely stating the property was “bank/corporate owned and not for rent” is posted in the window.

Status: Critical Event

🔴 August 25, 2022 | ADMINISTRATIVE | CRITICAL EVENT

Foreclosure Sale — Active Bankruptcy Stay in Effect

An attempted foreclosure sale is scheduled despite the active bankruptcy automatic stay. McCalla Raymer Leibert Pierce LLP (foreclosure counsel for Shellpoint Mortgage Servicing) proceeds to seek ratification, directly breaching the Bankruptcy Court’s Consent Order.

Status: Critical Event

🏛 July 9–10, 2023 | ADMINISTRATIVE

Register’s personal LLC, DR Enterprises LLC, turns on electricity at Plaintiff’s property for unauthorized occupants. BGE utility records addressed to “DR Enterprises, LLC” become key evidence. SDAT records confirm the entity.

MREC Complaint & $250,000 Guaranty Fund Claim Filed

Plaintiff files a formal complaint with the Maryland Real Estate Commission (MREC) against Register and Northrop Realty, alleging unauthorized leasing and management during an active bankruptcy stay. A $250,000 Guaranty Fund claim is filed simultaneously with 14 pages of evidence.

Register files an 88-page response.
